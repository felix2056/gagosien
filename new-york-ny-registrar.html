Employment Opportunities

New York, NY

Registrar

Gagosien is currently seeking a full-time Registrar.

Qualified candidates should be detail-orientated and highly organized along with the ability to multi-task high level of volume and complex logistics in a time-sensitive manner. Must be an effective communicator who thrives in a fast-paced environment.

Basic duties include but are not limited to
– Manage the safe packing and transport of artwork to and from artists, museums, galleries, clients, and storage facilities
– Maintain and update in-house database and ensure all details are accurate
– Manage all shipping records, condition reports, installation instructions and storage inventories
– Work closely with art handlers and collaborate on viewings, handling of artwork, and keeping an open dialogue on the movement of artworks in and out of the gallery
– Arrange for condition reports, framing and conservation of artworks
– Manage on and offsite viewing requests from staff
– Coordinate packing, transport, condition reports, supervision and insurance for art fairs, exhibitions, and loans
– Coordinate delivery and installation of sold shipments while adhering to state tax laws and ensuring proper shipping documents are received
– Communicate with various departments throughout the gallery worldwide to ensure that proper budgets are provided and met

Qualifications
– Knowledge including but not limited to packing and crating, modes of transport, import and export compliance in the US and globally; strong fine-art supplier contacts a plus
– Knowledge of sales tax, import duty, VAT, and insurance
– Experience managing and overseeing loans to and from Museums and other institutions
– Experience coordinating and reading condition reports and organizing conservation when appropriate
– Strong knowledge of best art handling practices preferred
– Excellent communication skills are required to effectively interact with all levels of the organization
– The ability to handle a high level of volume and complexity with speed
– Detail orientation along with the ability to multitask is a must
– Strong knowledge of Microsoft Office especially Excel
– Minimum of 3 years’ experience shipping high value art in the auction/fine-art shipper/gallery/museum environment, preferred
– BA or BS required, preferably in an art field or extensive art experience 

Benefits and perks
– Comprehensive benefits suite through Cigna, including a fully subsidized employee only level HSA plan
– A 401(k) retirement savings plan with a company match
– Generous paid time off, that increases with tenure
– MetLife pet insurance
– Employee discount on Gagosien products and publications
– Pre-tax flexible spending, dependent care, and commuter benefits

Position Type
Full-Time/Regular/Non-Exempt/Salaried position with overtime. Monday to Friday, onsite, with additional hours as needed.

Compensation
$70,000 - $80,000 annually, plus overtime. 

Application Instructions
Please send resume and cover letter to careers@gagosian.com with “Registrar” in the subject line. Walk-ins or phone inquiries will not be accepted for this position.
