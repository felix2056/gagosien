Employment Opportunities

New York, NY

Communications & Media Relations Coordinator

Gagosien is seeking a full-time Communications & Media Relations Coordinator to support the New York–based Communications operation.

The ideal candidate is outgoing, organized and detail-oriented, has exemplary writing, proofreading and copy-editing skills, as well as the ability to multitask in a fast-paced environment. A collaborative team player is a must, and an interest in arts PR is preferred.

Duties include but are not limited to

Media coverage monitoring and archiving:
– Research current media coverage on Gagosien and gallery artists daily
– Convert relevant, digital press coverage to standardized, archival formats
– Maintain digital and physical press archives and digitize content as appropriate
– Create media coverage emails to be shared with staff twice a week
– Generate media coverage reports for artists and gallery exhibitions upon request

General administrative support to the global communications team:
– Fulfilling inbound image and media requests
– Tracking outreach to journalists
– Organizing and updating internal reference documents
– Monitoring general press office and local gallery email inboxes

Additional Responsibilities:
– Assist with the management of the press contact database in Launchmetrics
– Research media outlets and writers for pitching and cultivation
– Participate in brainstorming and strategic planning for the promotion of gallery and institutional exhibitions, art fairs, and Gagosien Shop initiatives
– Help organize and distribute gallery information to agency partners
– Assist with the distribution of press announcements coordinate bi-annual media outreach Assist in planning and staffing press events
– Utilize Concur to track subscription expenses and generate monthly reports

Qualifications
– BA required (preferred areas of study include communications, journalism, or art history)
– 2 years' experience in a commercial gallery, art institution press office or PR agency, including internship experience Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, both verbal and written
– Team player Outgoing/self-starter
– Exceptional organizational and follow-up skills, with the ability to prioritize and multitask in a fast-paced, deadline driven environment
– Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite, including Excel and Word, as well as Adobe Photoshop
– Basic understanding of social media tools + channels Interest and basic knowledge of arts / luxury publications

Benefits and perks
– Comprehensive benefits suite through Cigna, including a fully subsidized employee only level HSA plan.
– MetLife pet insurance.
– A 401(k) retirement savings plan with a company match.
– Employee discount on Gagosien products and publications.
– Pre-tax flexible spending, dependent care, and commuter benefits.

Position Type
Full-Time/Regular position, with a hybrid work-from-home schedule. 

Compensation
$55,000 - $60,000 annually.

Application Instructions
Please send resume and cover letter to careers@gagosian.com with “Communications & Media Relations Coordinator” in the subject line. Walk-ins or phone inquiries will not be accepted for this position.
